Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 20, 2024

Alpha Seven Security Services ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website alphaseven.in or use our security services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name, address, and contact details
  • Phone number and email address
  • Property or business information for security assessments
  • Payment information for service agreements
  • Identification documents as required by security regulations

1.2 Website Usage Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website information
  • Device information and operating system

1.3 Service-Related Information

As part of our security services, we may collect:

  • Site security assessment data
  • Incident reports and security logs
  • Guard deployment and patrol information
  • Visitor and access control records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our security services
  • To respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • To conduct security assessments and risk analysis
  • To process payments and service agreements
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send important updates about our services
  • To ensure the safety and security of our clients and personnel

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our website and providing security services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Emergency Situations: With law enforcement or emergency services when necessary for safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure storage of physical records

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security service records as required by law

Security incident records are typically retained for a minimum of 5 years as per industry standards.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection to processing of your information
  • Data portability where applicable

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
